Training for beginners

Goals

Improve bone strength and density.

Benefits

Increased muscle mass and improved bone health.

Plan

Day 1 - Full Body Workout

  1. Squats - 3 sets of 8-10 reps
  2. Bench Press - 3 sets of 8-10 reps
  3. Deadlift - 3 sets of 8-10 reps
  4. Overhead Press - 3 sets of 8-10 reps
  5. Barbell Row - 3 sets of 8-10 reps

Day 5 - Full Body Workout

  1. Bench Press - 3 sets of 8-10 reps
  2. Overhead Press - 3 sets of 8-10 reps
  3. Barbell Row - 3 sets of 8-10 reps
  4. Bicep Curls - 3 sets of 10 reps
  5. Tricep Extensions - 3 sets of 12 reps

Day 2 - Rest

Day 6 - Rest

Day 3 - Lower Body Focus

  1. Squats - 3 sets of 10-12 reps
  2. Romanian Deadlift - 3 sets of 10-12 reps
  3. Lunges - 3 sets of 12 reps per leg
  4. Calf Raises - 3 sets of 15 reps

Day 7 - Lower Body Focus
