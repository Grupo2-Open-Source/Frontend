30-Day Strength Training Challenge

Structure

Week 1

Day 1 - Full Body Workout

  • - Squats: 3 sets of 8-10 reps
  • - Bench Press: 3 sets of 8-10 reps
  • - Deadline: 3 sets of 8-10 reps
  • - Overhead Press: 3 sets of 8-10 reps
  • - Barbell Row: 3 sets of 8-10 reps

Day 2 - Rest


Day 3 - Lower Body Focus

  • - Squats: 3 sets of 10-12 reps
  • - Romanian Deadlift: 3 sets of 10-12 reps
  • - Lunges: 3 sets of 12 reps per leg
  • - Calf Raises: 3 sets of 15 reps

Day 4 - Rest


Day 5 - Upper Body Focus

  • - Bench Press: 3 sets of 8-10 reps
  • - Overhead Press: 3 sets of 8-10 reps
  • - Barbell Row: 3 sets of 8-10 reps
  • - Bicep Curls: 3 sets of 12 reps
  • - Tricep Extensions: 3 sets of 12 reps

Day 6 - Rest


Day 7 - Full Body Workout (same as Day 1)

Week 2 to Week 4: Progression Weeks

  • - Increase the weight slightly each week (5-10% if possible).
  • - Maintain the same exercise structure, ensuring to listen to your body and rest as needed.

Final Days (Day 29 and 30): Challenge Yourself

Day 29: Full Body Max Out

  • - Test your one-rep max for squats, bench press, and deadlift.

Day 30: Rest and Recovery

  • - Focus on stretching and mobility exercises.
